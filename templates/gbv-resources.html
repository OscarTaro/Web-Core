Gender Based Violence Support

Emergency Contacts: National GBV Hotline: 080 80111

Legal Protection: Zimbabwe's Domestic Violence Act (Chapter 5:16) provides protection orders and legal recourse for victims of violence. It is crucial to know your rights and seek help when needed.

Services Available: Comprehensive support services including medical care, legal aid, counseling, and safe shelters are available through organizations like Musasa Project and Adult Rape Clinic. Remember, you are not alone, and help is available.

"No woman should live in fear of violence. Together, we can create a world where every woman feels safe and empowered." - Un Women

Rubatsiro Yemhiririro Yevanhukadzi

Nharembozha: 080 80111

Dziviriro Pamutemo: Mutemo weDomestic Violence (Chitsauko 5:16) unopa mirairo yekudzivirira uye nzira yekumhan'ara kune vanotambudzwa. Zvakakosha kuziva kodzero dzako uye kutsvaga rubatsiro kana zvichidiwa.

Mabasa Aripo: Rubatsiro rwakazara rwemishonga, rubatsiro rwemutemo, kupihwa mazano, uye pekugara zvakachengeteka kuburikidza nemasangano akaita seMusasa Project. Rangarira, hauzivi wega, uye rubatsiro rwuri kuwanikwa.

"Hakuna mukadzi anofanira kugara achitya mhirizhonga. Pamwe chete, tinogona kugadzira nyika iyo mukadzi wese anonzwa akachengeteka uye akasimba." - Unknown

Ukuxhaswa Kwezindaba Zobulili

Amakhokhi Wezimo Ezingalindelekile: 080 80111

Ukuvikelwa Ngokomthetho: Umthetho Wezindaba Zasekhaya (Isahluko 5:16) unikeza iziyalezo zokuvikela kanye nezindlela zokufuna usizo lomthetho kubantu abathintekayo. Kubalulekile ukwazi amalungelo akho nokufuna usizo uma kudingeka.

Izinsizakalo Ezitholakalayo: Ukunakekelwa kwezempilo, usizo lwezomthetho, izeluleko, kanye nezindawo zokuhlala eziphephile zikhona ngokubambisana nezinhlelo ezifana neMusasa Project. Khumbula, awuwedwa, futhi usizo lukhona.

"Akukho mkhaya okufanele uphila ngokuqhuba udlame. Ndawonye, singakha umhlaba lapho wonke umuntu ezwa ephephile futhi eqinile." - Unknown

Thikhodzo ya Vhulwane ha Vhathu

Nambara dza Khagala: 080 80111

Thivhudzo ya Mulayo: Mulayo wa Vhulwane ha Vhathu (Chapter 5:16) u nea thivhudzo na ndila ya u toda thuso ya mulayo. Zwi khou takalela u ḓivha u ḓivha na u ṱoḓa thuso.

Mabasa Aripo: U wanḓa u ḓivha, u ṱoḓa thuso ya mulayo, u ṱoḓa u ṱanganedza, na u ḓivha u ḓivha na masangano a saṅwaho a Musasa Project. Hu na vhukuma, u si na u ḓivha, na thuso i na.

"A si na mukadzi a fanela u ḓivha na mhiririro. U bva na u bva, ri nga kona u sika shango ḽi na vhukuma na u ḓivha." - Unknown

Religious Violence Support

Emergency Contacts: Zimbabwe Human Rights Commission: +263 242 703596

Legal Protection: Protected under Section 60 of Zimbabwe Constitution (freedom of religion). It is essential to stand against any form of violence that targets individuals based on their beliefs.

Services Available: Documentation of violations through Zimbabwe Peace Project, legal support from Zimbabwe Lawyers for Human Rights. Your voice matters, and reporting incidents can help bring about change.

"Injustice anywhere is a threat to justice everywhere." - Martin Luther King Jr.

Rubatsiro Yemhiririro Yechitendero

Nharembozha: Zimbabwe Human Rights Commission: +263 242 703596

Dziviriro Pamutemo: Inodzivirirwa pasi peChikamu 60 cheBumbiro reZimbabwe (rusununguko rwekunamata). Zvakakosha kumira kupikisa chero mhando yemhirizhonga inotarget vanhu maererano nezvitendero zvavo.

Mabasa Aripo: Kunyora zviito zvisiri pamutemo kuburikidza neZimbabwe Peace Project, rubatsiro rwemutemo kubva kuZimbabwe Lawyers for Human Rights. Izwi rako rinokosha, uye kutaurira zviitiko kunogona kubatsira kuunza shanduko.

"Kusagadzikana kupi zvako kunotyisidzira kugadzikana kwese." - Martin Luther King Jr.

Political Violence Support

Emergency Contacts: Zimbabwe Human Rights NGO Forum Hotline: +263 772 883 670

Legal Protection: Protected under Section 67 of Zimbabwe Constitution (political rights). It is vital to understand your rights and seek assistance if you face political violence.

Services Available: Medical and legal aid through Zimbabwe Association of Doctors for Human Rights. Your safety and well-being are paramount, and there are resources available to help you.

"The only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is for good men to do nothing." - Edmund Burke

Rubatsiro Yemhiririro Yezvematongerwo Enyika

Nharembozha: Zimbabwe Human Rights NGO Forum: +263 772 883 670

Dziviriro Pamutemo: Inodzivirirwa pasi peChikamu 67 cheBumbiro reZimbabwe (kodzero dzezvematongerwo enyika). Zvakakosha kunzwisisa kodzero dzako uye kutsvaga rubatsiro kana uchisangana nemhirizhonga yezvematongerwo enyika.

Mabasa Aripo: Rubatsiro rwekurapa uye rwemutemo kuburikidza neZimbabwe Association of Doctors for Human Rights. Kuchengetedzwa kwako uye hutano hwako zvakakosha, uye pane zviwanikwa zviripo kuti zvibatsire.

"Chinhu chimwe chete chinodiwa kuti chakaipa chitsvage, ndechekuti varume vakanaka vasadaro." - Edmund Burke
