Security Features

Your safety and privacy are our top priorities. Learn about the measures we take to protect your information.

End-to-End Encryption

All communications and data submissions are encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 protocols to ensure your information remains private.

Secure Data Storage

Your sensitive information is stored with AES-256 encryption and protected by multiple layers of security controls.

Anonymous Reporting

Choose to submit reports anonymously with no personal information collected or stored.

Legal Compliance

We strictly adhere to Zimbabwe's Data Protection Act and other relevant privacy legislation.

Two-Factor Authentication

Optional 2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account when accessing sensitive features.

Automatic Data Purging

Unnecessary data is automatically deleted after 90 days unless required for ongoing legal matters.

Your Role in Security

Create Strong Passwords

Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information.

Keep Credentials Private

Never share your login details with anyone, including ConnectAid staff.

Log Out After Sessions

Always log out from public or shared devices to prevent unauthorized access.

Update Contact Info

Keep your recovery email and phone number current in case we need to verify your identity.

Emergency Security Protocols

Immediate Threat Response

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@connectaid.org or call our 24/7 security line at +263 242 700 000.

Data Breach Notification

We will notify all affected users within 72 hours of discovering any potential data breach.

Legal Protection

All reports are protected under Zimbabwean whistleblower protection laws when applicable.

For more detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
